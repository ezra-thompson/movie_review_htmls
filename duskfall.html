Duskfall

In the haunting city of Liminalhaven, renowned detective Lucas Ravenwood (played by Sterling Caine) finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and darkness when a series of bizarre murders shakes the city to its core. As he delves deeper into the investigations, he discovers an uncanny link between the victims and a hidden supernatural realm that lurks beneath the surface of reality. With time running out, Lucas must confront his own demons and navigate the blurred boundaries between the living and the dead to catch the elusive killer.
