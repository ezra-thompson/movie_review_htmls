Fleshbound

In "Fleshbound," a group of unsuspecting medical students embark on a research project that delves into the darkest realms of body modification. Their insatiable curiosity leads them down a twisted path, where the boundaries between pleasure and pain, desire and horror, become gruesomely blurred. As their obsession deepens, they spiral into a nightmarish descent that challenges their sanity and morality.
