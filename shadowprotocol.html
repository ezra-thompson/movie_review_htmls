Shadow Protocol

In "Shadow Protocol," a former elite agent, played by Ryan Hunter, is pulled out of retirement when a dangerous international conspiracy threatens global security. Teaming up with a skilled hacker (portrayed by Emma Grey), he must navigate treacherous alliances, intense action sequences, and unravel the truth behind the shadowy organization that threatens to plunge the world into chaos.

  • Rating: 7.5/10
  • Length: 120 min
  • Premiere: 23 September 2023 (USA)
  • Category: Action/Thriller/Espionage
  • Director: Aiden Steele
  • MPAA: Rated PG-13 for intense sequences of violence, some language, and brief suggestive content
